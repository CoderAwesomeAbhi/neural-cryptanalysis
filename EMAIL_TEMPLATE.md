## Follow-Up Strategy

### If No Response After 1 Week:
Send a brief follow-up:

"Dear Professor [Name],

I wanted to follow up on my email from [date] regarding summer internship opportunities. I understand you receive many inquiries, but I wanted to reiterate my strong interest in your lab's work on [specific topic].

Since my initial email, I have [any new developments - paper acceptance, award, etc.]. I would be grateful for even a brief conversation about potential opportunities.

Thank you for your time.

Best regards,
Abhijay Gangarapu"

### If Positive Response:
Be prepared to discuss:
1. Your research in technical detail
2. Specific papers from their lab you've read
3. How you could contribute to their ongoing projects
4. Your timeline and availability
5. Your long-term research goals

## Key Points to Emphasize

1. **Independence:** You conducted this research independently (shows initiative)
2. **Rigor:** All experiments verified, reproducible, publication-ready
3. **Breadth:** Combines math, theory, and empirical work
4. **Honesty:** Acknowledged limitations and corrected errors (shows maturity)
5. **Impact:** Addresses fundamental questions in AI safety

---

## What NOT to Do

❌ Don't oversell or exaggerate
❌ Don't send generic emails (customize for each professor)
❌ Don't attach large files (link to GitHub instead)
❌ Don't be pushy or entitled
❌ Don't mention high school unless relevant (let work speak for itself)

✅ Do be professional and concise
✅ Do show genuine interest in their work
✅ Do highlight specific connections to their research
✅ Do be humble but confident
✅ Do follow up appropriately
